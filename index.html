1 Introduction

BlazingPeak Studio ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

By downloading or using any of our applications, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

Our applications may collect the following types of data:

  • Usage data: interactions with the app, session duration, features used.
  • Device data: device model, operating system, advertising identifiers (e.g. GAID on Android).
  • Advertising data: via Google AdMob, data is collected to display relevant ads (see section 4).
  • Purchase data: if you make in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment information.

3 How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Operate and improve our applications.
  • Display personalized or non-personalized ads via Google AdMob.
  • Analyze performance and fix bugs.
  • Process and validate in-app purchases (premium content, ad removal, etc.).

4 Advertising — Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob (Google LLC) to display advertisements in our applications. AdMob may collect and use data to personalize ads, including:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (GAID).
  • Your IP address (to determine your region).
  • Interactions with advertisements.

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings ("Google > Ads").
  • Reset your advertising identifier at any time.

To learn more about AdMob's privacy practices: policies.google.com/privacy

5 In-App Purchases

Some of our applications offer in-app purchases (premium content, ad removal, virtual items). All transactions are handled entirely by Google Play Billing.

We do not collect or store your credit card or payment information. Any billing-related questions should be directed to Google Play.

6 Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share certain data with:

  • Google AdMob: for advertising purposes (see section 4).
  • Google Play: to process purchases.
  • Competent authorities: if required by law.

7 Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Advertising data is managed and deleted according to Google AdMob's policies.

8 Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9 Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address listed in section 12.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The date of the last update will be shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
